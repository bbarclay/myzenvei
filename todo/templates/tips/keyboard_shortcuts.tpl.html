{t}You can switch to the 'Search' or 'Go' boxes quickly by using a special shortcut keystroke in your keyboard.{/t}

{t}Use the following shortcuts{/t}:

{t}ALT-3{/t} {t}(hold 'ALT' key and press '3' one time) - to access the 'Search' box{/t}

{t}ALT-4{/t} {t}(hold 'ALT' key and press '4' one time) - to access the 'Go' box{/t}
